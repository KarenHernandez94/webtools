Many Hobbies

Drawing

It started with drawing at the age of 5. I just remember picking up a pencil, scribbling on a white paper and a cartoon character appeared. In first grade, I was one of the only kids that knew how to draw cartoon characters. I was drawing a ton of Looney Tunes, Pokemon, and Digimon. At this age I knew I had talent but didn't know to what extent.

Art Classes

Once I got to middle school that is where I actually started taking art classes. One day after school I was near the courtyard, sitting on some steps, sketching an anime character. When the art teacher walks up to me and says "you need to be in my class"
